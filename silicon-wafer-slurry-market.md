<p><p>The silicon wafer slurry market is segmented into three primary types: first and second polishing slurries, and final polishing slurries. First and second polishing slurries are used in the initial stages of wafer processing to remove surface defects and achieve a smooth finish. Final polishing slurries are employed for ultra-fine polishing, ensuring minimal surface roughness and preparing the wafers for downstream manufacturing processes. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing wafer quality and performance in semiconductor production.</p></p>
