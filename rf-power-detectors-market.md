<p><strong>The RF Power Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>RMS Power Detectors</li><li>Non-RMS Power Detectors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RF power detectors market is divided into two main types: RMS power detectors and non-RMS power detectors. RMS power detectors measure the true power of an RF signal by accounting for variations in signal waveform, providing accurate readings for both continuous and pulsed signals. Non-RMS power detectors, on the other hand, offer simpler measurement methods that may not account for waveform variations, leading to less accurate results. Both types are essential for optimizing RF system performance across various applications.</p></p>
