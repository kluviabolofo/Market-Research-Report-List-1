<p><p>The Emergency Spill Response market comprises various product types designed to manage and mitigate spills effectively. Sorbents absorb or adsorb liquids, aiding in cleanup. Transfer products facilitate the safe movement of hazardous materials, and radio communication products ensure effective coordination during spill response. Other products may include personal protective equipment or containment systems.</p></p>
