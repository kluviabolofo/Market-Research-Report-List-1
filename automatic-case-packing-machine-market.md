<p><strong>The Automatic Case Packing Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>V-FFS</li><li>H-FFS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automatic case packing machine market consists of two main types: Vertical Form-Fill-Seal (V-FFS) and Horizontal Form-Fill-Seal (H-FFS) systems. V-FFS machines create packages vertically, efficiently filling and sealing products like snacks and powders, ideal for space-saving and high-speed operations. In contrast, H-FFS machines operate horizontally, handling bulkier items and providing a stable packing solution for fragile or irregularly shaped products. Both types enhance productivity and ensure consistency in packaging across various industries.</p></p>
