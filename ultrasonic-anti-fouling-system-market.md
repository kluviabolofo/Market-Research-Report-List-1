<p><ul><li>Open Water Transducer</li><li>Contact Transducer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultrasonic Anti-fouling System market features two primary types: Open Water Transducers and Contact Transducers. Open Water Transducers are designed to emit ultrasonic waves over a wide area, effectively deterring marine growth in large bodies of water, making them suitable for vessels or installations in expansive environments. In contrast, Contact Transducers are mounted directly on surfaces, providing localized ultrasonic protection. Both technologies aim to minimize biofouling without harmful chemicals, enhancing the efficiency and longevity of marine equipment.</p></p>
