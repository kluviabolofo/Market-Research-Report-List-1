<p><ul><li>Solvent Type</li><li>Powder Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV Curable Optical Fiber Coating market is categorized into two main types: solvent type and powder type. Solvent type coatings utilize solvents to dissolve the resin, facilitating easy application and durable adhesion, while powder type coatings involve applying dry powder that cures under UV light, creating a robust layer. Both types enhance fiber performance by providing protection against environmental factors and improving mechanical properties. Their choice often depends on application requirements, cost considerations, and production processes.</p></p>
