<p><p>The warehouse rack market includes various types to enhance space efficiency. Drive-in pallet racks allow forklifts to access products from one side, maximizing storage density. Push back pallet racks enable multiple pallets to be stored and retrieved easily, using a gravity-fed system. Cantilever racks provide support for long or awkward items, allowing for organized vertical storage. Other market segments may include selective racks, mobile racks, and pallet flow systems, catering to diverse warehousing needs and optimizing inventory management.</p></p>
