<p><p>The Plastic Leaded Chip Carrier (PLCC) sockets market is segmented into two main types: Through-Hole Technology (THT) and Surface Mount Technology (SMT). THT sockets are designed for mounting on printed circuit boards (PCBs) with leads that pass through holes, providing robust mechanical support. In contrast, SMT sockets are attached directly to the PCB surface, allowing for a more compact design and higher density. Both types cater to different application requirements, influencing factors like assembly process, space constraints, and overall performance.</p></p>
