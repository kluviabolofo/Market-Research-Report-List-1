<p><p>The C5 hydrogenated resin market is primarily segmented into two types: monomer and copolymer. Monomer resins are composed of single polymer chains, providing strong adhesion and clarity, making them ideal for applications like adhesives and coatings. Copolymer resins, on the other hand, feature two or more types of monomers in their structure, enhancing versatility and performance characteristics such as flexibility and thermal stability. Both types are essential in industries ranging from automotive to packaging, driven by specific application requirements.</p></p>
