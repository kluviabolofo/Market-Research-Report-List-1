<p><strong>The Remote Microgrid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Grid-Tied Type Microgrid</li><li>Independent Type Microgrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Remote Microgrid market consists of two primary types: Grid-Tied Microgrids and Independent Microgrids. Grid-Tied Microgrids are connected to the main power grid, allowing for two-way energy exchange, which enhances reliability and efficiency. In contrast, Independent Microgrids function autonomously, generating and managing their energy without grid reliance, ideal for remote or underserved areas. Both types serve to enhance energy resilience, integrate renewable sources, and support local energy needs while improving sustainability.</p></p>
