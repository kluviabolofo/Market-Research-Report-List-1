In-line inspection tools are integrated into production processes, providing real-time measurements and ensuring product quality during manufacturing. Off-line inspection, on the other hand, involves separate measurement setups where parts are tested after production, allowing for detailed analysis and quality checks without disrupting manufacturing flow. Both methods play crucial roles in maintaining precision and compliance in various industries, including automotive and aerospace.</p></p>
