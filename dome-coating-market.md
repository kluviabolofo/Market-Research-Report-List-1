<p><ul><li>Two Component Reactive Dome Coating</li><li>Solvent Based Dome Coating</li><li>UV Curable Dome Coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dome coating market is categorized into three main types. Two-component reactive dome coatings consist of a resin and hardener that chemically react to form a durable finish. Solvent-based dome coatings utilize organic solvents for application, offering versatility and quick drying times, but may have environmental considerations. UV curable dome coatings are cured through ultraviolet light, providing rapid processing and high durability, making them suitable for various applications. Each type serves specific requirements in protective and aesthetic applications.</p></p>
