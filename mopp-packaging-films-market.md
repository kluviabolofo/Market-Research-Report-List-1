<p><p>MOPP (Mono-Orientation Polypropylene) packaging films are categorized based on thickness into three main types: Below 50 μm, 51-100 μm, and Above 100 μm. Films below 50 μm are lightweight and ideal for applications requiring flexibility and clarity, often used in food packaging. The 51-100 μm category offers a balance of strength and puncture resistance, making it suitable for various packaging needs. Films above 100 μm provide enhanced durability and are commonly utilized in heavy-duty applications like industrial packaging.</p></p>
