<p><p>The Human Rabies Vaccination Market is categorized based on the types of cell cultures used in vaccine production. Vero Cell vaccines utilize African green monkey kidney cells, while BHK vaccines are derived from baby hamster kidney cells. Chick Embryo Cell vaccines are produced using fertilized chicken eggs, while Human Diploid Cell vaccines are made from human cells, offering a more antigenic response. The "Others" category includes alternative cell lines or novel production methods, contributing to diverse vaccine options for rabies prevention.</p></p>
