<p><p>The Industrial Belt Cleaning Systems market consists of primary and secondary belt cleaners, along with other related equipment. Primary belt cleaners are installed at the discharge point of the conveyor system to remove bulk material. Secondary belt cleaners follow, positioned further down the belt to catch residual material that primary cleaners may miss. Other market types include specialized cleaning devices or systems designed for specific applications, ensuring optimal conveyor performance and minimizing material carryback in various industrial settings.</p></p>
