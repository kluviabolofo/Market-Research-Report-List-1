<p><ul><li>P-PRP</li><li>L-PRP</li><li>L-PRF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) therapy utilizes concentrated platelets from blood to promote healing and tissue regeneration. The market features several types: P-PRP, which is a high platelet concentration used for orthopedic and aesthetic applications; L-PRP, characterized by lower platelet concentration, suitable for softer tissue healing; and L-PRF (Leukocyte-Platelet Rich Fibrin), which includes white blood cells and provides a slower, sustained release of growth factors, enhancing healing in various medical and dental procedures. Each type caters to specific therapeutic needs.</p></p>
