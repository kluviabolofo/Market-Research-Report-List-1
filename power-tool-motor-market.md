<p><p>The power tool motor market primarily encompasses two types: DC motors and AC motors. DC motors are favored for their portability and speed control, making them ideal for battery-powered tools. They provide high torque at low speeds, enhancing performance in various applications. In contrast, AC motors are typically used in corded power tools due to their efficiency and durability. They deliver consistent power and are better suited for heavy-duty tasks, making them a popular choice in industrial settings.</p></p>
