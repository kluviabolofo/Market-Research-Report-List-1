<p><p>The diaphragm wall equipment market is segmented into hydraulic and non-hydraulic types. Hydraulic diaphragm wall equipment employs high-pressure systems to create and stabilize deep excavations, offering efficiency and precision in large-scale projects. These systems are often used in urban environments where space is limited. Non-hydraulic diaphragm wall equipment relies on mechanical methods and manual labor for construction, making it suitable for smaller or less complex projects. Both types serve essential roles in foundation engineering and underground construction.</p></p>
