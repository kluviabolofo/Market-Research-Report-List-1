<p><strong>The Electromagnetic Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct-Acting Electromagnetic Valves</li><li>Sub-Step Direct-Acting Electromagnetic Valves</li><li>Pilot-Type Electromagnetic Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electromagnetic valves market comprises three primary types. Direct-acting electromagnetic valves operate directly with minimal pressure and are suitable for small flows. Sub-step direct-acting electromagnetic valves enhance precision control, allowing for variable flow rates with reduced energy consumption. Pilot-type electromagnetic valves, on the other hand, function using a small pilot signal to control a larger flow, offering greater efficiency and handling higher pressures. Together, these valves cater to various industrial applications, enhancing automation and fluid control systems.</p></p>
