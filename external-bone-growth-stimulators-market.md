<p><ul><li>Combined Magnetic Field (CMF) Devices</li><li>Capacitive Coupling (CC) Devices</li><li>Pulsed Electromagnetic Field (PEMF) Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>External bone growth stimulators are specialized devices used to enhance the healing of fractures and bone injuries. The Combined Magnetic Field (CMF) devices utilize a combination of magnetic fields to stimulate bone growth. Capacitive Coupling (CC) devices employ electrical fields to promote osteogenesis by applying low-frequency currents. Pulsed Electromagnetic Field (PEMF) devices generate pulsing electromagnetic fields to enhance cellular activity and improve healing. Each type offers distinct mechanisms for stimulating bone regeneration and addressing various orthopedic conditions.</p></p>
