<p><strong>The Silver-Platinum Paste Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Curing Paste</li><li>Sintered Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silver-Platinum Paste market comprises two primary types: curing paste and sintered paste. Curing paste is used for applications requiring a soft, flexible bond that solidifies upon heat application, commonly utilized in electronic components for enhanced conductivity and thermal management. Sintered paste, on the other hand, involves a process where particles are fused at high temperatures, creating a durable and conductive layer ideal for demanding applications such as solar cells and advanced microelectronics, ensuring efficiency and longevity in performance.</p></p>
