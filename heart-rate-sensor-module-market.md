<p><p>The Heart Rate Sensor Module market is primarily divided into two types: Photoplethysmography (PPG) sensors and Electrocardiography (ECG) sensors. PPG sensors utilize light absorption to detect blood volume changes, making them ideal for non-invasive monitoring in wearables. In contrast, ECG sensors measure electrical signals generated by heartbeats, providing detailed cardiac information often used in medical settings. Both types cater to various applications, including fitness tracking, health monitoring, and clinical diagnostics, enhancing the understanding of cardiovascular health.</p></p>
