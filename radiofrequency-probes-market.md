<p><ul><li>Bipolar RF Probe</li><li>Monopolar RF Probe</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radiofrequency probes market includes two primary types: bipolar and monopolar RF probes. Bipolar RF probes use two electrodes located close together, allowing for precise tissue targeting and minimizing damage to surrounding areas, making them ideal for delicate procedures. Monopolar RF probes, on the other hand, utilize a single electrode connected to a grounding pad, enabling deeper tissue penetration and broader ablation areas. Both types serve critical roles in minimally invasive surgeries and therapeutic applications across various medical specialties.</p></p>
