<p><p>The cardiac ablation system market is categorized into multielectrode and single-point systems. Multielectrode systems utilize multiple electrodes to deliver energy across a larger area, allowing for more comprehensive treatment of arrhythmias and potentially reducing procedure times. In contrast, single-point systems focus on delivering energy at a specific location, providing targeted treatment but may require multiple applications for broader arrhythmia management. Both types are pivotal in enhancing the efficacy and safety of cardiac ablation procedures.</p></p>
