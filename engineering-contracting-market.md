<p><p>The engineering contracting market consists of various segments, primarily general contracting and subcontracting. General contracting involves a central contractor overseeing a construction project, managing all aspects from planning to execution. This type typically assumes full responsibility for project delivery. Subcontracting, on the other hand, involves specialized contractors hired by the general contractor to perform specific tasks, such as electrical or plumbing work. This division of labor allows for expertise in specialized areas while streamlining project management and execution.</p></p>
