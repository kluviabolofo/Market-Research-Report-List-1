<p><p>The atomic spectrometer market includes various types such as Atomic Absorption Spectrometry (AAS), X-ray Diffraction Spectrometry (XRD), and X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometry (XRF). AAS is utilized for analyzing metal concentrations by measuring the absorption of light. XRD is essential for determining crystal structures and material properties by analyzing diffraction patterns. XRF provides qualitative and quantitative information about elemental composition by measuring emitted X-rays from a sample. Each technique serves distinct applications across industries like materials science, environmental monitoring, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
