<p><strong>The Offshore Cable Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inter-array Cable</li><li>Export Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The offshore cable market primarily consists of two types: inter-array cables and export cables. Inter-array cables connect individual wind turbines within an offshore wind farm, facilitating the transfer of generated electricity to a central point. Export cables, on the other hand, transmit the electricity from the offshore wind farm to the onshore grid, often covering longer distances. Both types are critical for the efficient operation of offshore wind farms, ensuring reliable energy delivery and contributing to renewable energy development.</p></p>
