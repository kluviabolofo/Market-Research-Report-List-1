<p><p>The olive oil market is primarily categorized into three types: Extra Virgin Olive Oil, Olive Oil, and Olive Pomace Oil. Extra Virgin Olive Oil is the highest quality, extracted from the first cold pressing of olives without chemical processing, offering rich flavor and health benefits. Regular Olive Oil blends refined oil with a small percentage of virgin oil, providing a milder taste. Olive Pomace Oil is derived from the leftover olive pulp after extraction, processed with solvents, typically used in cooking and industrial applications.</p></p>
